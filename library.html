ADSR

Attack – the beginning of the sound; Decay – a fading sound; Sustain – how long it can hold; Release – the fade to silence. The elements form the ‘envelope’ that describes the shape of a sound.

Feedback

A high-pitched screech caused when a loop of sound is caused by a signal passing through an amplifier to a microphone and back again.

Gain

How loud a signal is before it goes through an amplifier. Can be another word for volume, and another word for guitar distortion.
